Senior Living

Come back to life in full spirits

The care and warmth when you need it the most.

The concept of senior living broadly encompasses individualized, tailored care structure for a hassle-free, safe and need-based retire environment of your choice. From independent living, assistive living to palliative care, post operative care and short stays, Sundaratheeram offers comprehensive and need-based senior living solutions for a blissful silver years.

With the help of passionate professionals and 24×7 availability of staff to tend to you, residents in Sundaratheeram’s communities experience personalized, round the clock service to make their retire years golden. With Sundaratheeram Senior Living, you have the advantage of giving your wife, retirement from household chores, thus making it a complete retirement for the family.

WHY Senior living

age friendly design

Our residents want to live an active and healthy lifestyle. However, we understand the challenges that ageing presents, and build our homes in anticipation of those challenges. A Sundaratheeram Senior Living home is designed to have all the comforts of a regular home with additional functionality and detailing to suit your needs.

Active Life

At Sundaratheeram Senior Living, we design activities and amenities that help you lead this active life. Energise your daily routine with a badminton match or long walks.

Safe and Secured

With over ten years of experience in Senior Living projects, we have all the systems in place to ensure maximum safety and security of our residents. In all these years, we have a record of zero break-ins in our projects.Your care and safety is our top priority.

Hassle-free Living

We have a jam-packed roster of maintenance services offered by our in-house maintenance staff. Electricians, plumbers and repairmen are available on call. All essential services such as garbage disposal, cleanliness of common areas, and regular maintenance of infrastructure are handled by our team.

Assisted Living

Come back to life in full spirits

The care and warmth when you need it the most.

In today’s fast-paced world, families may find it difficult to cope with such situations which require an awful level of attention. Taking care of an elderly parent or relative can be a truly testing situation. That's why we have come up with this residency option-' Assisted Living'. It's a long term care option. Here we take care of your housing, essential services and need-based health care. Special team is on the ready to take care of Meals, Medication management/assistance, exercises/activities, and daily activities including dressing, bathing and in-house transportation, based on your requirement.

When your father, mother or a close relative falls ill, it becomes a difficult situation to deal with. Even after they had their medical procedure done, you will have to take extra care so as to keep them safe from infections and/or accidents due to negligence. Your home could be your best option but the kind of dedicated professional care an elderly patient needs, that you can't provide. You can't keep them in the hospital also.

If the patient is suffering from memory loss/Alzheimer's disease/ Parkinson's syndrome, special care will be required. For that purpose, we have trained bystanders available to stand by the patient round-the-clock and a 24 Hrs. medical team is available for any emergency that may arise. Also, at Sundaratheeram Assisted Living residencies, we have a dietician who will decide your dietary plan, in accordance with your doctor's report. Our services extend to 24×7 security, housekeeping and maintenance, laundry, yoga, meditation and a geriatric gym with a dedicated trainer, etc.

INDEPENDENT Living

Reclaim your life

Rediscover the joy of living.

Times have changed. People are finding it difficult to spend time with their elderly parents. With their children being away with their families, many seniors in Kerala are living lonely lives. It is a really difficult situation for older people. Independent living at Sundaratheeram is meant for people like them. Here, we have dedicated staff to help seniors who are alone: - they will take them to walking, community activities, or help them in whatever way they need it. Loneliness alleviation is the key purpose of that team.

Our Independent living facilities are custom-designed for seniors. The rooms are spacious and doorways are built for easy navigation. 'Panic Button' is a standard feature for any emergency. Round-the-clock help is available for everyday activities, including professional medical assistance and other senior based requirements. With ample avenues available for social activities and hobbies, these homes are a great choice for seniors who are alone and struggling to cope with loneliness. Independent living is best suited for those who need no or little assistance, but want a retirement home for their immediate family, far away from the hassles of everyday life.

PALLIATIVE CARE

The strength to endure
a difficult ordeal

Support to tide over those testing times.

No one wants to stay long at the hospital, yes, that’s the truth. But, when somebody has undergone a major medical procedure, it’s not wise to go back to home early. Once someone undergoes a major medical procedure, they'd definitely require specialised care. When they come back to home, the scenario becomes difficult to deal with-you must be always careful, so as not to inflict any infections and never to impart any psychological stress on the patient.

Unexpected things could happen: - situations may arise when you may need a doctor's attention in the middle of night. Yes, it'll be a heavy deal on your family. Moreover, it's impossible to provide the patient with hospital-like professional care.

That is why we've decided to have a Palliative Care special housing scheme at our community. It's a specialized care system designed for senior citizens with serious illnesses. It focuses on providing relief from the pain or discomfort. Another key aspect is relieving them of the stress of the illness by improving the quality of life for the patient and their loved ones around them. Communities, along with support groups play a crucial role in helping seniors with their quality of life, at all stages of the illness. Our Palliative Care unit takes care of patients with chronic illnesses such as cancer, cardiac diseases, kidney failure, Alzheimer’s disease, Parkinson’s disease and many such serious illnesses.

POST-OPERATIVE CARE

Hospital - like care.
Home - like ambience.

Homely care and warmth with professional support.

It’s a truly challenging time when someone close to you undergoes a medical procedure. Yes, it’s an unfortunate but unavoidable scenario. Post-operative care could be a nightmare for regular families. It takes a lot of patience, energy to take care of somebody who has undergone a major surgery. Any infection or accident due to negligence may result in dangerous consequences. The love, care and affection of your family is not enough for a patient recovering from a major surgery.

At Sundaratheeram, we've a specialty-built post-operative care residency system for seniors, which allow them to stay close to hospitals, in a homely surrounding for a short duration of time or till recovery. Doctor-on-call is a standard feature available round-the-clock. Combining this feature with home-like warmth and care makes this facility a godsend gift for seniors who are recovering from a major surgery.

In some cases, post-operative care refers to assistance in pain management, wound care and recovery, depending on the type of surgery one has undergone. Post-operative care also involves assessment, diagnosis, planning, intervention and outcome evaluation depending on the pre-surgical health status, type of surgery, and the individual’s needs. Sundaratheeram's assisted living extends to post-operative care.

SHORT-STAYS

Experience home,
away from home.

Feel like home: with friends, family and lot of fun.

This facility is not senior-specific. Anyone could avail Sundaratheeram short-stays at a reasonable price. This is especially beneficial for NRIs who are visiting their parents for a considerable duration, say one or two months. Staying with older parents and children at a village house can be uncomfortable for people who've used to life abroad. Also, staying in a hotel for months can never be an economical option. Besides, a hotel is not great place for children and seniors. Therefore we have introduced the short stays facility at Sundaratheeram. You could rent out an apartment for whatever duration you may like and you could make use of all the amenities available. Sure this is a unique offer you must have never heard of!

When it comes to seniors, Sundaratheeram short stay programs benefit individuals with long-term care needs. It's a good option for seniors who are recovering from surgery or those who needs a change of place for a while in a retirement community. Decide your duration of stay: over a weekend, a month long break or just an escape from the hustle of the city, Sundaratheeram has a place for you. Easily accessible from the city, the place is brimming with activity. Come over and find lots of like-minded people in their silver years. Sundaratheeram is inviting you to come over and stay as long as you want, it's a great place to stay!
