Aid & Support

Essential Services

Our Essential services include a Senior Friendly Housing at a Convenient Location with an Active Community with proper Food & Dining. All these foods are Nutritionist prescribed.

Emergency Services

24×7 ambulance, systemized evacuation procedure, on call doctor, availability of 24×7 paramedical staff within the community, and emergency arrangements with hospitals.

Basic Services

Our basic services include 24×7 security personnel, timed daily day visits for single and special residents, housekeeping and cleaning services, pest control, property and rental management, to mention a few.

Comfort Services

Our communities come with a geriatric gym with a qualified trainer, yoga and meditation facilities, spiritual, wellness and nutrition programs, beauty and salon facilities, convenience kiosk and grocery shop, ATMs, library, business centre and a fully serviced guest house.

Lifestyle Services

Sundaratheeram also offers concierge services, battery cars operated within the community, travel desk services with more detail to senior specific tours, celebration of birthdays, anniversaries and multi-religious festive celebrations

Advisory Services

Sundaratheeram Senior Living offers wealth management, legal and documentation management services in addition to geriatric counselling services.
